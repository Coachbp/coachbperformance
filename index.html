I'm a USA based worldwide Sports Psychology Consultant who brings something rare: I've actually lived the elite athlete experience. I pair that lived experience with formal training in sports psychology and behavioural science — plus an approachable style that helps athletes open up and do real work. I'm also a mother of four competitive kids, giving me a unique lens on the family dynamics that shape athlete mindset.
